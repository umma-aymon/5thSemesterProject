Our Story

This Restaurant is a dream come true for a bunch of friends sharing their sheer love for food andexperimentation.
One of it's kind in the port city of Chittagong, we opened our doors to you on the 1st of September,2020.
Our location inspires us to celebrate the amazing seafood that this city has to offer.
We are constantly experimenting with both local and international flavors and constantly pushing boundaries whilst also maintaining quality and hygiene to the highest standards.
We are grateful for all the love you have given us so far.
We hope to continue serving you with amazing, mouthwatering dishes for you, your friends and your family.
